Who we are:

Munder Difflin Co. is a stationery and lifestyle brand based in South Park, Colorado founded and owned by husband and husband team, Mr.Garrison and Mr.Slave. The company officially launched via theRealMunderDifflin.com in November 1986. What began as a small business based out of Mr.Garrison's modest 5story mansion has quickly grown into an international brand over the few decades.
Munder Difflin Co.'s roots are in stationery, but as we continue to grow and develop more products under the Munder Difflin Co. brand, we look forward to expanding the breadth and depth of our product line. We've also collaborated with partners like fox, hulu, and southpark studios, to name a few.We strongly believe life's personal stories and moments are best told through the gift of a handwritten card or note, and hope that our goods inspire you to share these moments with others. Please let us know if there's anything we can assist you with or ways that we can improve your shopping experience.

Mr.Garrison:
As co-founder and Creative Director, Anna Bond's illustrations and designs have molded Rifle Paper Co.’s unique aesthetic. Born and raised in Summit, New Jersey, Anna got her bachelor’s degree in Graphic Design and made her way to Florida to work as an Art Director at a magazine. After a few years of magazine and freelance illustration work, she fell in love with stationery and began working toward developing a line of products. As Creative Director, Anna designs Rifle Paper Co.’s collection as well as oversees the creative direction of the brand including product development, business strategy, and marketing. She was also recently honored as an ADC Young Gun and is featured in Grace Bonney's book In the Company of Women
Mr.Slave:
Mr.Slave is co-founder and CEO at Rifle Paper Co. Originally from Orlando, Nathan left college early to become a full-time musician and toured in a band for nearly five years before leaving to partner with Anna to develop her growing stationery business. Nathan’s role is key to cultivating business relationships, managing quality manufacturing, finances, and overseeing the operations of the company. He was recently named to Forbes magazine's 30 Under 30​ list of young game changers, movers and makers.
